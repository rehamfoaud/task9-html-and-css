Ratatouille

rat with an extraordinary sense of smell and taste. Unlike other rats, Remy dreams of becoming a great chef, inspired by his idol, Chef Gusteau, a famous French chef who believes that "anyone can cook Remy faces a big challenge, as rats are usually not welcome in kitchens. However, fate leads him to a restaurant in Paris, where he meets a young and inexperienced garbage boy named Linguini. When Linguini accidentally mixes up ingredients and creates a dish, Remy secretly helps him by controlling his movements from under his chef's hat, turning Linguini into a skilled cook. Aemy and Linguini work together, they face many challenges, including trying to keep their secret hidden from the restaurant's other staff, especially the strict Chef Skinner. Eventually, Remy proves that anyone, regardless of their background, can achieve their dreams if they have passion and dedication

Encanto

The story begins in Colombia, where the Madrigal family lives in a magical house, and each family member has a special power given to them by the magical spirit of the mountains. For example, Isabela, Mirabel's sister, can make flowers bloom, while Luisa, another family member, has incredible physical strength. However, Mirabel, the main character, doesn't have any magical powers, which makes her feel left out compared to the rest of the family. On her 15th birthday, they discover that the magical powers of the family are starting to fade, and their enchanted house is at risk. Mirabel embarks on a journey to discover the cause of the magic's decline and how she can save her family. Throughout this journey, she learns that love, family, and self-acceptance are more important than any magical gift. In the end, it is revealed that every member of the family is important, including Mirabel, who finds her own strength in strengthening the family bonds.

Frozen

The story begins with Elsa, who has magical powers that allow her to control ice and snow. When she was a child, she used her powers to play with her younger sister, Anna, but one day, Elsa accidentally hurt Anna with her magic. To protect Anna, their parents decide to keep Elsa's powers a secret, and the two sisters grow apart, with Elsa being taught to hide her abilities. As Elsa grows older, she becomes the Queen of Arendelle. During her coronation, she accidentally reveals her powers, plunging the kingdom into an eternal winter. She flees to the mountains, where she builds a magnificent ice palace and isolates herself from everyone. Anna, not knowing how to fix the problem, embarks on a brave journey to find her sister and save the kingdom. Along the way, she is joined by Kristoff, an ice harvester, his reindeer Sven, and Olaf, a snowman created by Elsa. In the end, Anna discovers that true love is what can break the eternal winter. The love between the sisters is revealed to be the key to ending the curse Elsa put on the kingdom.

Esjera

One day, a strange merchant arrived in the village and was captivated by the shimmering leaves of the tree. He attempted to cut a branch to take the leaves and sell them for a fortune. Suddenly, a strong wind blew him away. The villagers realized the tree was protecting itself and began to wonder: Why is this tree special? Why has it stood here for hundreds of years? The Secret of the Tree: One night, a little girl named Layla, known for her love of nature, dreamed that the tree spoke to her. The tree told her that its roots reached deep into a magical spring of water that kept it alive. This spring was the source of the village’s prosperity, ensuring their lands were fertile and protecting them from drought. The tree warned Layla to tell the villagers not to harm it or attempt to cut it down, as its presence was vital to the village’s well-being.
